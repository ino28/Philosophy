## *(The) Torah*

aka

- 摩西五经(Christian), 梅瑟五书(Catholics), Five Books of Moses
- Pentateuch(Christian, "five scrolls")
- the Law, 律法书(Christian)

---

#### Genesis 创世纪
- "Creation", "In the beginning"

#### Exodus 出埃及记
- "Exit", "Names"

#### Leviticus 利未记
- "Relating to the Levites", "And He called"

#### Numbers 民数记
- "In the desert [of]"

#### Deuteronomy 申命记
- "Second-Law", "Things" or "Words"
